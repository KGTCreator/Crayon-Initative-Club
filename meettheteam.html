Founder & President Of Crayon Initative:


Bryan Ware

Bio: Bryan is both the creator of Wareworks, and a counsuling firm specializing in product design, packaging, and manufacturing processes. He started the initative after going to a restaurant with his family, wondering where the crayons his two boys were using went once they left. After discovering that they end up in landfills, he began to find a way to re-pourpose the crayons into forms that gave more joy to the world. And from there, it all began.
Occupation: Leader and Founder of the Crayon Initative
