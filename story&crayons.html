Our Story

This club did not start when the original organization began. It wasn't until 2021 that this club was convieced for the first time. On that day, Abigail Marder concieved the idea of a club that would be able to help with the well being for people of any age. With this objective in mind, She aproached Web Design teacher Sarah Leonard with the idea.


The two soon thought of an idea to collect large amounts of crayons, and somehow ship them to children's hospitals across the nation. It was this, that they discovered the Crayon Initative itself, and so began a club to donate crayons to their cause and help supply children's hospitals with new crayons for their patients to use. And now here we are!
